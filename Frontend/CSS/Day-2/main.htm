Shorthand examples & notes

Shorthand margin/padding: margin: top right bottom left or margin: vertical horizontal or margin: all

Border shorthand: border: 2px solid #000 (width style color)

Box-sizing difference:

  • content-box - width/height apply to content only.
  • border-box - width/height include padding + border.
Inspect tool quick tips: press F12 or Ctrl+Shift+I, then select the element picker (top-left) and click the demo box. Look at Computed panel to see the box model diagram.
